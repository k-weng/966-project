Welcome to our study!

IMPORTANT! This study runs on a desktop/laptop.

The study will NOT run on a mobile device.

You need to be focused to do this experiment, please make the browser window as big as you can and avoid distraction.

Multiple Object Tracking

You will see several moving geometrical objects presented in the center of the screen.

There will be also a background image surrounding the objects.

You are supposed to keep track of some of the objects and ignore the others.

The target objects will blink before the animation starts.

When the animation stops, please click on the objects that you were tracking. The study is expected to take XXX minutes.
